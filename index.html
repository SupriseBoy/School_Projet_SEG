About us

Title

Welcome to Tutor Inc. an online help center for those in need.

Whether you are a first year student, or a fourth year who's got it pretty much figured out, sometimes everyone needs a little hand with their homework.

No worries! That's why you are here.

Take a glance at our available courses, and see for yourself how much we can help.

Title

Unlike other services like Khan Academy or Chegg, we provide a live connection with a tutor!

This is because, as fellow students, we know how much better (and easier!) it is to study with someone else.

Check out our student forum to see if the question you have is being worked on right now!

Title

For now, Tutor Inc. is only available for students studying at the University of Ottawa, but we are working hard to expand our reach.

If you are a student at the University of Ottawa, feel free to contact anyone of our team with any questions you may have!

Find our contact info in the Team section at the bottom of the page!
